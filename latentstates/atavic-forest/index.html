Atavic Forest

2022–2023 · Metamersion · Mucho Flow Festival
A 45-minute live audiovisual performance. The forest exists wholly in latent space—each frame generated in real-time by neural networks trained on over 180,000 forest images. The boundary between the biological and the computational dissolves into undergrowth. Collective movement in the room steers the journey; sonic vibrations perturb the generative networks themselves. The forest responds to sound as much as motion, each viewer experiencing their own singular path through ancestral memory.

This forest has been fully dreamt by Artificial Intelligence. Hidden within the corpus of its training data lie forgotten memories that diffuse through a fluid continuum. Throughout our evolutionary path, our genes have kept a record of our relationship with nature, hiding it in order to avoid unnecessary expression. This installation is an expression of that hidden knowledge. An exploration of what a space of these memories look like if we could directly interface with them in a pre-linguistic stance; direct contact with alterity.

Deep inside Atavic Forest
The Experience
Thirteen key frames from the generative performances. Each frame rendered live from the latent space, shaped by the collective presence and sonic environment of the room.
How It Works
The Atavic Forest is a journey through ancient psychographic spaces rendered using generative adversarial networks and latent blending techniques. Neural networks process the collective body movement in the room via IR tracking cameras, and audio reactivity comes from a 7.2 surround system that feeds directly into the generative layers. The result is a singular experience for each group—the forest you see is shaped by where you stand, how you move, and the sounds you make. No two performances are the same.
