About

Davide Crombie is a postdoctoral researcher who joined the Mainen Lab in 2024. His research focuses on visual thalamic circuits, particularly examining how corticothalamic feedback and behavioral state modulate neural activity in the dorsal lateral geniculate nucleus across multiple timescales. In the lab, he contributes to projects involving the International Brain Laboratory and studies of LSD effects on neural population dynamics.
