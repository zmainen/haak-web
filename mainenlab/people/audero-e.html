About

Enrica Audero was a postdoctoral researcher in the Mainen Lab from 2011-2014, where she contributed to research on serotonergic systems and behavior. Her work focuses on the role of serotonin in regulating anxiety, aggression, sleep-wake cycles, and autonomic functions, with particular expertise in optogenetic manipulation of dorsal raphe serotonin neurons and the function of serotonin 1A receptors. She has since departed the lab and her current institutional affiliation is not available.
