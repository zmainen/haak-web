About

Steve Macknik was a postdoc in the Mainen Lab from 1999-2001, working during the lab's early years at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory. His research focuses on visual perception, eye movements, and the neuroscience of illusions, with notable work on microsaccades, perceptual expertise in radiology, and the intersection of neuroscience with art and magic. He is currently a Professor at SUNY Downstate.
