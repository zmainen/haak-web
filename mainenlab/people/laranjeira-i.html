About

Ines Laranjeira is a PhD student in the Mainen Lab, co-supervised by McNamee, studying serotonin and brain-wide modulation since 2017. Her research focuses on understanding how serotonin shapes neural dynamics and decision-making processes, with recent work examining serotonin's modulatory effects in neural subspaces orthogonal to choice-related activity. She has contributed to the lab's collaborative efforts in developing standardized approaches for measuring decision-making in mice and advancing computational methods for analyzing complex learning behaviors.
