About

Gautam Agarwal was a postdoctoral researcher in the Mainen Lab from 2001-2019, where he contributed to the lab's research program over nearly two decades. His research focused on spatial coding and neural field dynamics, particularly studying how local field potentials in the hippocampus encode spatial information and investigating olfactory-guided navigation through spatial mapping in piriform cortex. He has since departed the lab and his current affiliation is not listed.
