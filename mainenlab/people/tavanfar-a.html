About

Alireza Tavanfar was a postdoc in the Mainen Lab from 2020, focusing on theoretical physics research spanning quantum mechanics, holographic theories, and gravitational physics, with particular expertise in black hole physics and quantum field theory. He is currently a Visiting Scientist in the lab, continuing his research at the intersection of quantum theory and gravity. His work explores fundamental questions about unitarity, quantum information, and the holographic principle in various gravitational contexts.
