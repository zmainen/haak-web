About

Cindy Poo was a postdoc in the Mainen Lab from 2011-2022, where she contributed to research on olfactory processing and COVID-19 screening approaches using household odorants. Her research focuses on olfactory system function, including spatial coding in piriform cortex during navigation, cortical feedback control of olfactory circuits, and the neural basis of odor representations. She is currently a Senior Scientist at the Allen Institute.
