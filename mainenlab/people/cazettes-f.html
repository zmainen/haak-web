About

Fanny Cazettes was a postdoc in the Mainen Lab from 2016-2022, where she worked on facial expression decoding projects that contributed to understanding how behavioral expressions reflect underlying cognitive states in mice. Her research spans computational neuroscience and decision-making, with particular expertise in auditory processing and spatial localization derived from her extensive work on barn owl sound localization systems. She is now a tenured researcher at CNRS in France, where she continues her work on neural mechanisms of perception and decision-making.
