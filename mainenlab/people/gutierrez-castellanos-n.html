About

Nicolas Gutierrez-Castellanos was a postdoc in the Mainen Lab from 2016-2018, where he worked on serotonin circuits. His research focuses on the neural mechanisms underlying sexual behavior and reproductive cycles, with particular emphasis on hypothalamic and limbic circuits that control female sexual rejection, male copulatory behavior, and hormonal modulation of neural plasticity. He has also investigated cerebellar motor learning, chemosensory processing in the amygdala, and the maturation of cortical inputs to brainstem neuromodulatory centers.
