About

Ninglong Xu was a postdoc in the Mainen Lab from 2006-2008, during the laboratory's early years at the Champalimaud Centre for the Unknown. His research focuses on neural circuit mechanisms underlying perceptual decision-making, with particular emphasis on cortical and striatal circuits that support sensory categorization and flexible behavior. He is now a Principal Investigator at the Chinese Academy of Sciences, where he continues to investigate how neural ensemble dynamics in motor and sensory systems contribute to adaptive decision-making processes.
