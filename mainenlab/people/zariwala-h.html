About

Hatim Zariwala was a PhD student in the Mainen Lab from 2000-2007, where he worked on olfactory decision-making during the lab's early years at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory. His research focused on understanding how animals process odor information to make behavioral choices. He has since departed from research.
