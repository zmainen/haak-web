About

Guido Meijer was a postdoctoral researcher in the Mainen Lab from 2018-2024, where he contributed to projects on serotonin and brain-wide modulation as well as the International Brain Laboratory collaboration. His research focuses on understanding neural correlates of decision-making and multisensory processing, with particular expertise in large-scale electrophysiological recordings and standardized behavioral paradigms in mice. His work spans brain-wide neural activity mapping, reproducibility in neuroscience methods, and the integration of visual and auditory information in cortical circuits.
