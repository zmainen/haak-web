About

Eric Lacosse was a postdoc in the lab from 2022-2024, bringing expertise in neuroimaging methods and computational approaches to brain data analysis. His research focused on developing statistical methods for fMRI analysis, predicting brain activation patterns from resting-state data, and improving the reproducibility of neuroimaging studies. He is now a Staff Scientist in the Digital Therapeutics Programme.
