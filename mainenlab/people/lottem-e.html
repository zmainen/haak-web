About

Eran Lottem was a postdoc in the lab from 2011-2018, where he worked on serotonin optogenetics and computational decision theory. His research focused on understanding how serotonin neurons modulate behavior, particularly in the context of decision-making, cognitive flexibility, and foraging strategies, combining optogenetic manipulations with behavioral paradigms. He also contributed to studies on sensory processing across multiple modalities, including somatosensory and auditory systems.
