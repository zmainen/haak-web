About

Sara Matias was a PhD student in the lab from 2008-2015, where she worked on serotonin optogenetics projects, investigating the activity patterns and dynamics of serotonin neurons and their role in cognitive flexibility. Her research combined optogenetic techniques with fiber photometry to reveal how serotonergic signaling influences behavior and mechanosensory responsivity. After completing her PhD, she continued research on reinforcement learning and dopamine signaling, though her current position is unknown.
