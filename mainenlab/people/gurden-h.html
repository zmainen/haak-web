About

Hirac Gurden was a postdoc in the Mainen Lab from 2000-2005, where he worked on olfactory bulb circuitry. His research focuses on the intersection of olfaction with metabolism and cerebral blood flow, particularly in the context of obesity and diabetes, using optical imaging techniques including speckle imaging and two-photon microscopy. His current affiliation following his departure from the lab is unknown.
