About

Dhruba Banerjee was a postdoctoral researcher in the lab from 2017-2018, working on serotonin optogenetics projects. His research focuses on the neural mechanisms underlying behavioral persistence and foraging decisions, particularly examining how serotonin neuron activation influences active exploration and exploitation strategies in probabilistic tasks. He has since departed the lab and his current affiliation is unknown.
