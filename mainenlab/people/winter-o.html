About

Olivier Winter served as a technician in the Mainen Lab from 2018-2020, where he contributed to the International Brain Laboratory project. His research focuses on developing computational tools and methods for large-scale electrophysiology, including spike sorting algorithms, data architecture systems, and quality metrics for neural recordings across different brain regions and species. He has since departed from the lab and is currently unaffiliated.
