About

Mattia Bergomi was a postdoc in the lab from 2016-2020, where he worked on serotonin fMRI projects. His research spans computational topology, machine learning, and their applications to neuroscience and music analysis, with particular expertise in topological data analysis and persistence methods. His current affiliation following his departure from the lab is not known.
