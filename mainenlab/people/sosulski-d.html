About

Dara Sosulski worked as a Research Assistant in the Mainen Lab from 2003-2005, during the lab's early years at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory. Her research has focused on olfactory system function, examining how odor information is processed and represented across different brain regions, with particular attention to circuit dynamics in the piriform cortex and orbitofrontal cortex. Her work has also explored how perturbations to olfactory maps affect odor discrimination and the normalization mechanisms in olfactory bulb output.
