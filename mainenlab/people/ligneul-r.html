About

Romain Ligneul was a postdoc in the Mainen Lab from 2017-2022, where he worked on projects investigating serotonin and novelty. His research focuses on computational approaches to understanding decision-making, controllability, and social hierarchies, with particular emphasis on how neural systems process uncertainty and regulate exploratory behavior. He is now a Principal Investigator at INSERM in France.
