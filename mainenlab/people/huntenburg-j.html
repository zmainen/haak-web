About

Julia Huntenburg was a postdoc in the Mainen Lab from 2018-2021, where she contributed to the International Brain Laboratory project investigating neural mechanisms of decision-making across multiple brain regions. Her research spans computational neuroimaging methods, cortical organization, and large-scale brain connectivity, with particular expertise in developing tools for high-resolution neuroimaging analysis and understanding functional gradients in cortical structure. She has since departed the lab, though her current affiliation is not publicly available.
