About

Magor Lorincz was a postdoc in the lab from 2009-2013, where he worked on serotonin optogenetics projects. His research focuses on thalamic and cortical circuit dynamics, particularly in sleep-wake regulation and epilepsy, with expertise in electrophysiology and closed-loop brain stimulation approaches. He is now a Principal Investigator at the University of Szeged.
