About

Veronica Egger was a postdoc in the Mainen Lab from 2000-2004, where she worked on olfactory bulb circuitry. Her research focuses on the cellular and synaptic mechanisms underlying olfactory processing, with particular emphasis on granule cell dendritic integration, reciprocal synapses, and neuromodulatory influences on olfactory bulb function. She is currently a Professor at Regensburg University, where she continues her work on olfactory system neurobiology.
