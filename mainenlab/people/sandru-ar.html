About

Adrian Razvan Sandru is a postdoctoral researcher in the Mainen Lab who joined in 2020, bringing an interdisciplinary background spanning computer vision, machine learning, philosophy, and law. His research in the lab focuses on perceptual decision-making and sensory processing, particularly studying confidence in auditory perception and cross-modal interactions in flavor perception. Prior to neuroscience, his work encompassed deep learning applications for face recognition and pose estimation, as well as philosophical investigations into ontology and phenomenology.
